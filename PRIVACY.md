# Privacy Policy for TypeFlow

Last updated: March 8, 2026

## English

TypeFlow is designed to process typing input on the device whenever possible.

### Information We Collect

TypeFlow does not create user accounts and does not intentionally collect personal identity information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

The app may locally store limited data on your device in order to provide the product experience, including:

- Keyboard setup status
- Purchase or unlock status
- Free usage count
- Local settings and preferences

This data is stored locally on your device or within Apple's system services, such as StoreKit and Keychain, and is used only to make the app function correctly.

### Typing and Translation Data

TypeFlow is built to process typing and translation on-device. Your typing content is not uploaded to our servers for cloud processing.

If Apple services are involved for in-app purchases, Apple may process information required to complete the transaction, restore purchases, or validate entitlements according to Apple's own privacy practices.

### Network Access

TypeFlow may access the network only when needed for App Store purchase-related functions, such as:

- Loading in-app purchase product information
- Completing a purchase
- Restoring a previous purchase
- Confirming an existing purchase through Apple's services

TypeFlow does not use your typing content for advertising or third-party tracking.

### Third-Party Services

TypeFlow does not include third-party advertising SDKs or third-party analytics SDKs.

The app may rely on Apple-provided services, including:

- StoreKit for in-app purchases
- Apple system security and storage services

### Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We do not share your typing content with third parties for advertising purposes.

### Your Choices

You may stop using the app at any time and remove the app from your device. Data stored locally by the app is generally removed when the app is deleted, except where Apple system services retain information as part of purchase history or device backups.

### Contact

If you have privacy questions about TypeFlow, you may contact:

- ET Wang
- GitHub: https://github.com/ETWang1991/SuperKeyboard

## 中文

TypeFlow 在设计上尽可能于设备本地完成输入与翻译处理。

### 我们收集的信息

TypeFlow 不创建用户账户，也不会主动收集姓名、邮箱、手机号、精确位置等个人身份信息。

为了实现产品功能，App 可能仅在你的设备本地保存少量数据，包括：

- 键盘启用状态
- 购买或解锁状态
- 免费额度使用计数
- 本地设置与偏好

这些数据仅保存在你的设备本地，或保存在 Apple 提供的系统服务中，例如 StoreKit 和 Keychain，仅用于保证 App 正常运行。

### 输入与翻译内容

TypeFlow 的输入与翻译处理以本地完成为目标。你的输入内容不会上传到我们的服务器进行云端处理。

如果涉及 Apple 的内购服务，Apple 可能会按照其自身隐私政策处理完成交易、恢复购买或验证购买状态所必需的信息。

### 联网情况

TypeFlow 仅会在与 App Store 购买相关的必要场景下访问网络，例如：

- 加载内购商品信息
- 完成购买
- 恢复历史购买
- 通过 Apple 服务确认已有购买状态

TypeFlow 不会将你的输入内容用于广告或第三方追踪。

### 第三方服务

TypeFlow 不包含第三方广告 SDK，也不包含第三方统计分析 SDK。

App 可能使用 Apple 提供的系统服务，包括：

- 用于内购的 StoreKit
- Apple 提供的系统安全与存储服务

### 数据共享

我们不会出售你的个人数据。

我们不会为了广告目的与第三方共享你的输入内容。

### 你的选择

你可以随时停止使用本 App，并从设备中删除。App 保存在本地的数据通常会在删除 App 后移除；但 Apple 系统服务可能仍会按其机制保留购买记录或备份中的相关信息。

如果你对 TypeFlow 的隐私政策有疑问，可以通过以下方式联系：
